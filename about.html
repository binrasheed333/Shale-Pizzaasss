Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Shale Pizza

Q. Do you offer gluten-free pizza options? ▼

Yes! We offer gluten-free crust for an additional Rs 200. Please inform our staff about any allergies when ordering.

  • Gluten-free thin crust available
  • Separate preparation area
  • Dedicated gluten-free toppings
Q. Can I customize my pizza with extra toppings? ▼

Absolutely! Each pizza can be customized with extra toppings:

  • Extra cheese: Rs 100
  • Extra meat toppings: Rs 150
  • Extra veggies: Rs 50
  • Special sauces: Rs 80
Q. What are your delivery charges? ▼

Our delivery policy:

  • Free Delivery: Orders above Rs 1500 within 5km
  • Standard Delivery: Rs 100 for orders below Rs 1500
  • Minimum Order: Rs 800 for delivery
  • Delivery Time: 30-45 minutes
Q. Do you offer catering for events? ▼

Yes! We provide comprehensive catering services:

  • Office Meetings: Pizza platters from Rs 3000
  • Birthday Parties: Custom packages
  • Corporate Events: Bulk order discounts
  • Contact us 48 hours in advance
Q. Are your ingredients fresh and local? ▼

We use fresh, high-quality ingredients:

  • Daily fresh vegetables from local markets
  • Fresh dough made every 4 hours
  • Imported Italian cheese and olive oil
  • No preservatives or artificial flavors
Q. What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept multiple payment options:

  • Cash on Delivery
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Bank Transfer
  • EasyPaisa
  • JazzCash
  • Online Payment
